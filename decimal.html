Fraction To Decimal


To convert fraction to decimal we have to follow these steps


1. If the fraction is 2/5 and we have to convert it to decimal we have to look at the denominator. The denominator is 5 now we have to see what we can multipy to 5 to get 10 or 100.


2. 5*2 = 10 so we change the denominator to 10. We have to also multipy the same number with the numerator. So the numerator becomes 4 because 2*2 = 4.


3. Now that we have another fraction 4/10 we can convert it into decimal. We can convert it to decimal by looking at the denominator which is 10.


4. In 10 we have one 0 so we will put the decimal point before one number but 4 is a one digit number so the answer will be 4.0.
